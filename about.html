About Me

A little history about me, I have 20 years of experience in Auto-Collision Repair Technology, and I have 6 years experience in 3d-Modeling, Animation, Robotics, and Information Technology. I have many useful skillsets to contribute to several industries, with proficiency in many design programs and craftsmanship of traditional fabrication techniques, I am capable of delegating tasks and time needed to complete large projects at any stage. I strive to improve my planning, improve efficiency, and I have the propensity to be resourceful. During my time time in college, I spent my first two years at SNHU studying Information Technology: Robotics and Artificial Intelligence where I began learning computer building, and software development. I learned the fundamentals of scripting Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS and later C++ on an extracurricular Arduino projects. I changed majors halfway through my degree into Graphic Design: 3D Modeling and Animation where I was eager to learn a lot of design programs, earning my bachelor's degree in May of 2022. I earned a Latin honors “cum laude” with a 3.617 GPA, respectfully. I was thrilled to learn from Southern New Hampshire University that I had been nominated, and later through leadership coaching inducted into the National Society for Leadership and Success (NSLS) in 2021. The NSLS is a national honor society whose Greek motto is "Sigma Alpha Pi". This milestone opened my eyes to what I could achieve with the right mindset. With a touch of good mentorship, I make an exceptional leader. I enjoy taking a leading role in developing a contribution to my community.
